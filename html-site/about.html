Letter from our Founder

Building Futures, One Life at a Time

"When we founded Humanity Horizon in 2015, we had one simple conviction: that geography should never dictate a child's destiny. We set out to build programs that solve the root causes of systemic poverty — education, healthcare access, and environmental safety."

"A decade later, we've walked alongside 50,000+ individuals as they unlocked their potential. That's 50,000 children in school, families with safe drinking water, and women leading businesses. But our work is far from finished."

"We promise absolute transparency. 85% of your support goes directly into field operations, and every year we invite external auditors to publish our financials. Thank you for joining us in building hope."

Priya Sharma

Our Journey

A Decade of Real Change

15
2015

Foundation Established

HHF officially registered in Mumbai with primary mission mandates under MH/2015/0042.

17
2017

Mobile Health Clinics

Deployed first mobile medical vans to remote regions in Odisha and Rajasthan Thar Desert.

19
2019

Thar Desert Clean Water Initiative

Commissioned first 50 solar-powered water tubewells supplying permanent drinking water.

21
2021

Digital Learning Packs

Supplied tablets, local offline pedagogy packs, and network hubs for 500+ rural girls.

24
2024

FCRA & Transparency Milestone

Achieved official GuideStar Platinum transparency ranking and FCRA registrations.
